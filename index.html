Welcome to my page!

Hello, my name is Sofie Piper. I am currently a sophomore communications and political science student enrolled at Virginia Tech.

This site is built to help incoming communication students understand and apply the six principles of good design and the four laws of Gestalt Theory. Each tab on the left names a principle or theory which you can click on to receive more information about. My contact information is written below, in case you have any questions.

This page has been edited from its original state. Additional pictures have been taken with a DSLR camera and added as examples to five different pages. These pages are balance, continuity, contrast, focal point, and similarity.
